Private Lessons

Sounds Abound Music offers private in-home guitar, ukulele and drum lessons throughout the Northwest Philadelphia region at very competitive rates. Think of the convenience and time saved by having an instructor come to your home. It's not uncommon for a parent to spend an hour of their day driving to and from the music store while also having to wait for their child's lesson to finish. Add in the possibility of traffic or inclement weather and you run the risk of being late or missing the lesson entirely. With private in-home lessons, the lesson begins when the instructor arrives at your house.... and you are never charged for a lesson the instructor is unable to make. There is also no long term obligation or tuition fees often associated with other music teaching facilities. Sounds Abound Music works on a month to month basis and allows for lesson cancellations - free of charge - when needed. So give us a call and let's get started!
